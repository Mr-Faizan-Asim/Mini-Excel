    <h1>Mini Excel README</h1>

    <p>Welcome to Mini Excel, a simple web-based spreadsheet application.</p>

    <h2>Features</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Create, edit, and delete cells in a grid-based interface.</li>
        <li>Perform basic mathematical calculations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division).</li>
        <li>Save and load your spreadsheet data.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>How to Use</h2>
    <p>Using Mini Excel is straightforward:</p>
    <ol>
        <li>Click on a cell to select it. Selected cells are highlighted.</li>
        <li>To edit a cell, double-click on it, and a text input field will appear.</li>
        <li>To perform a calculation, type your formula into the cell (e.g., <span class="code">=A1+B1</span>).</li>
        <li>Press Enter or click elsewhere to apply the calculation.</li>
        <li>To save your work, click the "Save" button.</li>
        <li>To load a saved spreadsheet, click the "Load" button and select your saved file.</li>
    </ol>
